Task:   Logging In to the IWM system.   

Description:   Allows users to log-in to the IWM system and gain access to the areas they have been authorized to use.    
   
1.Upon loading the IWM URL the Log In screen will be displayed.  
 
 
 
2.Type your User Name and Password  
 
3.Select the "Login" button to proceed      
 
   The IWM Home Page will be displayed.
