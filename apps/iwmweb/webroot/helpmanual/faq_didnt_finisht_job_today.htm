Did not finish the job today, what to do?
Previous  Top  Next

Symptom: A worker was not able to complete a job assignment within a same shift / day.

1.A worker who starts working in a Job assignment must record the time spent on that job (see "recording time for a job").  
 
Comment: This will ensure that the worker is given credit for his time spent on the job. For as long as the job remains open (worker does not closes the job in Mobile Worker) the system will continue  

2.   Once a worker has recorded time for a job, the job will continue to be assigned to the same person shift-after-shift until the worker finishes the assignment and closes the job.
