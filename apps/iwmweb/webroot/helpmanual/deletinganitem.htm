Task:   Deleting an item in IWM   

Description:   Allows a previously saved item in IWM to be removed from the system.

Hint: Some items may not be deleted from the system or may have specific requirements for deletion. There are two major reasons that will prevent deletion from the system:  
 
A) if the item has been used by the system for previous job creations (ex. An object has jobs in history) or  
 
B) if other items are directly related to the object you are trying to delete (ex. An organization that has employees associated with it).      


1.From a list of items locate the one you want to remove.      
 
2.Select the "delete" link to the right of the item.  
 
The item will be deleted, unless there are other conditions prohibiting this, in which case you will be notified.
