Task:   Adding an new Locator in IWM.
   
Description:   Allows users to add a new locators in the location hierarchy in the system.

1.Select the "Add Locator" button from the "Locator List" screen.  

This will display the "Add Locator" screen.  
 
     

2.   Select the appropriate level of the Locator Hierarchy in which you would like to create a new "Locator".

Select one level higher in the hierarchy than the level of the locator you wish to create. Thus, selecting nothing will create a locator at the highest level of the hierarchy. Selecting the first level will create a locator in the second level (a subcomponent of the level above).  

Note: If you had selected a level in the hierarchy during the previous screen (Locators screen), this will be carried over into the "Add Locator" screen.      
 
3.Complete the required fields.  

·The "Name" is the full length name of the Locator.  
 
·The "Abbreviation" is what will be displayed on the field to workers performing work.  
 
·The "Address" is the physical location and is not required; it is intended for support information.  
 
·The "In Service" indicates the date in which the locator was put into service, as defaulted to today's date.  
 
·The "Urgent E-mail" if a worker request is made and it is flagged for an E-mail to be sent it will go to the E-mail that is put in here.  
 
 
4.Select the "save" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
