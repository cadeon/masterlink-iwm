Description:    merge with Calender

The "Schedule" subsection in the system represents a snapshot of the work scheduled to a person through the system. It serves as a reporting tool, not only review the current day's assignments for any person in the system, but also past assignments.

One key functional feature of the "Schedule" is the capability to "Reset" a person's schedule, this allows managers to remove any assignments from this person and ask the system to rebuild a new schedule for that person.
