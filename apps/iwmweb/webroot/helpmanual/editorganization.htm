Task:   Editing an existing organization in IWM.

Description:   Allows users to edit the information stored for an organization in the system.    

1.Select the "edit" link to the right of the organization's name.  
 
2.Selecting the "edit" link will display the "Edit Organization" screen.  
 
3.Modify or Enter the information you wish to edit.  
 
 

4.   Select the "save" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.

   The system will then display the "Organizations" screen.
