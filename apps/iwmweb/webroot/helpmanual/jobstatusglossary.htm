Job Status

Indicates the present state of a job.  

Available Job States  
 
·Not Yet Available: [image]  
 
·Initial Null State: the initial state of all jobs before the "Job State Manager Agent" process it.  
 
·Ready for Scheduling: indicates the job is ready to be assigned to a candidate.  
 
·Scheduled Pending Dispatched: indicates that the job has been assigned to a candidate.  
 
·Dispatchable: Pending Dispatch, indicates that the worker has received the assignments but has yet to open this specific job.  
 
·Dispatched Job Order: indicates the job has been received and oped by a worker in the field.  
 
·Job Completed: indicates that the job has been closed by the worker.  
 
·Canceled, Inactive: indicates that the job has been canceled before being completed.  
 
·Waiting for Precedes: indicates the job is part of a sequence, and is pending completion of preceding jobs.
