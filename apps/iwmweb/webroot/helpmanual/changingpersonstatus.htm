Changing Worker Status
Previous  Top  Next

Task:   Changing the status of a worker in IWM.
   
Description:   Allows users to change the status of a worker from "Active" or "Inactive" in a person in the system. Upon creation in the system all persons are defaulted to active. Active workers are considered eligible to receive work orders from the system. Workers may be deactivated to indicate, for example, that they no longer work for the organization. In cases were a worker is not available for work for a short period (vacation, sick leave, etc.) see Edit worker.

1.Select the Define - Worker.  
 
From the "worker" screen use the organization filter in which to shorten the list of worker to find the worker you wish to edit.  
 
2.Select the "edit" button located on the "Edit" column.
