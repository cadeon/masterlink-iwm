Description:   

The "Worker" subsection contains the lists of people related to an organization and employees participating in the system. People not participating in the system's automation may be included in this list as a reference without disturbing the process. This is possible by having "active" and "inactive" states for people. Participating people are considered working employees by the system. This is the section of the system where one may adjust their calendar to indicate weather they are going to be available to work and at which site location. This is also the section were one can inspect what jobs has an employee been assigned today and in the past.

Maintaining an accurate calendar will ensure that the system optimizes your organization to its true potential and avoids scheduling mistakes. The Calendar is one of the critical sets of information the system uses to optimally plan and schedule work by matching the right available employee for the required work.
