Task:   Deleting a Worker in IWM.   

Description:   Allows users to delete workers.   

1.Select the Define - Worker.  
 
2.Locate the Worker.  

From the "Worker" screen shorten the list by using the organization filters in which the person you wish to delete is located.  
 
 
3.Select the "delete" link located on the far right column of the list of workers.  
 
 
 
If the person is eligible for deletion, a "delete" link will be displayed on the furthest right column of the table.  
 
If the person is not eligible deletion, then they may only be changed in status to "Inactive".  
 
Hint: If a person is not eligible for deletion, it is probably because he has: (1) an active calendar, (2) has work scheduled for today, or (3) has worked on scheduled work in the past.  
        
      If #'s 1 or 2 are correct remove all calendar days from this person, and clear his schedule for  
      today. This should enable deletion.  
 
      If # 3 is true, the person may not be deleted as his information is part of the systems historical data archive.
