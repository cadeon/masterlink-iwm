Task:   Selecting an item    

Description:   IWM uses "buttons" and "links" to enable user interaction. Depressing either one will execute the action represented in their label, or direct the user to the destination represented in their label.

1.Locate the button or link you desire.  
 
To commit a change or transaction, select the appropriate button from the selection provided in the screen you are interacting with.  
 
2Move your cursor over the desired "Button" or "Link"  
 
3Depress the left button from your mouse to activate.  
 
Hint: "Buttons" will change appearance and "Links" will change color or the cursor change shape in order to confirm your that have correctly position the cursor over them. Depress the left button on your mouse to activate.
