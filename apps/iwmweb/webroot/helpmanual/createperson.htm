Task:   Adding a Worker in IWM.   

Description:   Allows users to add people that work and place them into an organization in the system.    

1.Select the "worker" link under the worker column.  
 
From the "Define - worker" screen look through the fillers to find the organization you wish to add the Worker to.  
 
2.Select the "Add Worker" button.  
 
From the "Worker" screen locate and select the "Add Worker" button located on the bottom and top left corner of the list of workers. This will display the "Add Worker" screen.     
 
3.Fill in the required information.  
 
 

4.   Select the "next" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.    

5. Fill in the skills of the worker that has been added.

6. Select the "next" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.

7. Select the "Check Box" (add pic) that will indicate the "Rights" (add pic) that the worker will be allowed to do.

8. Select the "Finish" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
