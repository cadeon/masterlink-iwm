Task:   Accessing a worker's day Schedule in IWM.   

Description: Allows users to view and reset today's schedule (jobs assigned to worker) in the system.    

1.Select the Organization.  
 
From the "Worker" screen use the organization filter to help find Worker you wish to view.  

2.Select the "Calendar" button in the "Clndr" column for the Worker whose schedule you which to review.  
 
3.Select the "Jobs" link "" for the schedule you wish to see from the list.  
 
The list of jobs on that schedule will be displayed in the "Jobs" panel.
