Mobile Worker

Description:   

Mobile Worker is the field component of the IWM system. Through this sub-section, person's participating in the system receive their work assignments. All work assignments include the location and description of the work to be performed. In addition the format in which work is defined in both the Templates and Implementation sections serve as "field job-aids". This is, the Actions defined in those sections are presented as chronological lists of instructions (actions) needed for the completion of the task. Through the Mobil Worker sub-section participating persons are able to record any field observations and time spent on the task. This Information is readily available for managers to review via the IWM Jobs or Project Jobs sub-sections, or through predefined reports once a worker records his/her progress.
