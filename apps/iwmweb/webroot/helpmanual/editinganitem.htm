Task:   Editing an item in IWM
   
Description:   Allows previously saved items in IWM to be modified.    

1.From a list of items locate the one you want to edit.  
 
2.Select the "edit" link to the right of the item.  
 
The "edit" screen will be displayed.  
 
3.Modify the fields you desire.  
 
4.Select the "save" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
