Tasks

Description:
   
Tasks within an Object are the subcomponents that specify the requirements of that object. Tasks are usually inherited directly from the Object Template although they may be created or customized specifically for one object in particular. Tasks are composed by at least one action that form a list of requirements that must be met for that task. (i.e. to-do list).
