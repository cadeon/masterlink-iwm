Task:   Using Filters in IWM   

Description:   Filters help narrow down large lists of items by being more specific.


1.Select a topic from the filter drop-down menu.  
 
To use a filter start from the top drop-down menu of the filter panel. Selecting a category from the top will show sub-categories in the drop-down menu below, and so forth.  
 
 

2.   The system will automatically execute the filter criteria.

Steps 1 may be repeated as many times as necessary to narrow or expand your list.  
 
Note:The system will not limit the number of items shown on a list, which may results in needing to page over to find items not being shown on 1st page. To obtain these records on the 1st page you must narrow down your search using additional filters.
