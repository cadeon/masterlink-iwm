Worker Status
Previous  Top  Next


Description:   

A worker's "Status" is used to indicate the state of participation in the system. An "active" status indicates the person is participating in the system and may receive work assignments through it. An "inactive" status indicates that the worker is not participating because he/she is no longer a member of the organization or is not participating in the system. The worker's status is also important due to the systems safeguards for historical records. The system will not allow the deletion of persons that have participated in the system in the past (have received work) in order to keep accurate records of participation. In these cases the worker's status is used to remove no-longer participating members of the organization.
