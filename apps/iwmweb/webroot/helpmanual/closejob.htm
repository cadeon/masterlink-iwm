Task:   Closing a job in the Mobile Worker section of IWM.
   
Description:   Allows users to close a Job that has been completed or no longer valid in the Mobile Worker section of IWM.    

1.Identify the job you with to close located on the "Mobile Worker Screen".  
 
 
 
2.Select the "Close" button for the job you with to close. The Status will change from "Open" to "Closed" to confirm
