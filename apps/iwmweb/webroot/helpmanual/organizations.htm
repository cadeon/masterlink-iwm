The Organizations sub-section is part of the Requirements section of IWM and deals human resources (i.e. the human requirements). In this section, information about the organizations' personnel is stored for use by the system in planning and scheduling of work. Based on the information entered here, the system will match the best available human resource to the requirements of the work to be done.

The criterion for having multiple organizations in the system is arbitrary to the specific application environment and how the user wishes to manage work. Some characteristics used to make this choice may be a physical location, a particular skill set, organization hierarchy or other choice of classification based on the users needs.

Some of the information recorded in this section are: the Organization's details, such as location and type (i.e. vendor, contractor; if more than one is being managed); and details of employees participating with the system, such as ID (i.e. name or employee number), skill type and level, billing rate, location, security level. Sub-menus of Organization's are: Worker.
