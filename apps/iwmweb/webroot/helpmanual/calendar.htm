Description:   

The Calendar section of IWM is the system capability that allows its automation agents to plan, schedule and prioritize work and assign it to the most appropriate available human resource. Maintaining an accurate calendar will ensure that the system optimizes your organization to its true potential and avoids scheduling mistakes. The Calendar is one of the critical sets of information the system uses to optimally plan and schedule work by matching the right available employee for the required work. The Calendar subsection allows users to show when and where a human resource will be available to work. Thus this information must represent what is actually happening in the organization (i.e. Update the calendar when a person is sick, or changing a working location).
