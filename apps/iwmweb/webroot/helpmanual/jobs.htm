Description:   

Jobs is a command section where the management of work orders takes place (the only exception are Project Jobs). This is the sub-section where the activity defined in both the Requirements and Implementations sections takes place in the form or work orders. It is all handled automatically by the system from creation, planning, scheduling, assignment, and tracking of work. While the system is capable of carrying out all these activities, system users may override the automated agents and manage the work according to changing environmental conditions or expert judgment.
