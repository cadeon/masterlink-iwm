Task:   Deleting an existing Locator in IWM.
   
Description:   This feature will allow the user to delete a locator.


1.Filter the hierarchy of locators to find the locator you desire to delete in the "Locator List" screen.  
 
2.Select the "Trash Can" to the far right under delete.  
 
3.Select the "Ok" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
