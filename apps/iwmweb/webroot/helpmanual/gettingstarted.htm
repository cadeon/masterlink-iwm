The Intelligent Work Management System is divided into five (5) operational sections. Each section is represented by a number indicating the order in which activities are conducted in a process of organizational improvement.

1.Section 1: Define  
 
The Define section contains the components that will define how your organization will be run. In short they define, What needs to be worked on; Who is going to perform the work; and Where work is to be performed.  
 
Sub-Topics: Worker, Organizations, Locators, Templates  

2.   Section 2: Implement

The Implement section contains the components that allow the activation of the policies established in the Define section. In short they identify, What in specific needs to be work on (instances of items defined in Templates; and What sequential procedures should take place).  
 
Sub-Topics: Objects, Project, stencils  
 
3.Section 3: Control     
 
The Control section contains the components that allow the daily monitoring and management of activities through the system. In short they identify, What work needs to be done; Who is doing the work; What stage of completion a series of jobs is in; and What feedback has been given from work.  
 
Sub-Topics: Jobs, Projects, Mobile Worker, Maintenance Request, Reports  
 
4.Section 4: Measure  
 
The Measure section is not currently active. This section will include components for the tracking of performance in multiple perspectives and summaries (Reports)  
Sub-Topics: Reports, Something  
 
5.Section 5: Analyze  
 
The Analyze section is not currently active. This section will include components that will allow for the analysis of organizational performance via simulation capabilities.  
Sub-Topics: Test, Menu item
