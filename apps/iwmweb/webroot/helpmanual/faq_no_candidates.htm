No Candidates for a job
Previous  Top  Next

Symptom: There are no workers shown as candidates for a job.

Troubleshooting steps:

1.Note the Date, Location (highest level locator), Skill and Skill Level for that job in the "Schedule Job" properties.  
 
2.Verify that there is at least one worker (or as many as need for that job) who has the same skill and equal or greater skill level as that required for the job.  
 
Comment: The system will only match those workers who meet the requirements for a job.  

3.   Verify that the worker/s have a calendar day for the Jobs date.

Comment: If the worker's calendar does not show "today", the system assumes the worker is not available to work.  

4.   Verify that the location for the worker/s calendar is the same than that for the job.

Comment: If the worker's calendar does not show the same location as the job, the system assumes the worker is not available to work at that location/site.
