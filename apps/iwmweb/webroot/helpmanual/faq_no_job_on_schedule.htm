No Jobs on schedule
Previous  Top  Next

Symptom: There are no jobs shown in mobile worker for that worker.

Troubleshooting steps:

1.Verify that the worker has been scheduled to work today (see calendar), and verify that there are jobs assigned to him/her (see schedules).  
 
2.Verify that there is a "schedule date" displayed.  

Comment: If there is no date showing, then "today" is not scheduled in the worker's "calendar". Verify that the worker has a calendar day scheduled for "today" or create a new schedule.  

3.   Verify that the displayed "date' and "schedule date" are the same.

Comment: If the dates are not equal it means the schedule shown is outdated, possibly due to the worker not "ending-shift" on a previous day.  

4.   Verify that the displayed "schedule date" is today's date.

Comment: If the dates are not equal it means the schedule shown is outdated possible due to the worker not "ending-shift" on a previous day.  

5.   Verify that the worker is scheduled for today in their "calendar".

Comment: If the worker's calendar does not show "today", the system assumes the worker is not available to work.
