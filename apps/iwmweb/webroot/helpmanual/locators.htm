Description:   

Locators are another subsection of the Define section. Locators represent a hierarchy of locations in the users' work environment. They are an important component in the definition of where work may take place, and where objects in the system may be located. The exact hierarchy structure and the level of detail may be customized to the needs and requirements imposed by the system's users and should be well planned and remain consistent for optimal system operation.
