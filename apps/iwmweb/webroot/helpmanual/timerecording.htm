Task:   Recording or editing Time for a Task in the Mobile Worker section of IWM.
   
Description:   Allows users record or edit their Time spent on a Task in the Mobile Worker section of IWM.    

1.Select the "Tasks button" for which you wish to record or edit Field Conditions in the Mobile Worker screen.  
 
This will display the "Job Tasks" screen.  
 
 

2.   Select the "Actions" button for the task you wish to record or edit Field Conditions.
 
This will display the "Task Actions" panel.  
 
 
 
3.Locate the "Time" field which you wish record Time.  

Use the text field provided to record your time.  
 
     
 
4.Select the "save" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
