Two workers have the same job
Previous  Top  Next

Symptom: Two or more workers receive the same job in the field.

Troubleshooting steps:

1.Note the number of people required for that job in the job properties.  
 
Comment: It is possible for two or more workers to receive the same job assignment if the job requirements indicate so.  

2.   Verify that all workers have a current schedule and are viewing a current schedule (see here).    

Comment: A worker who is observing an old assignment schedule may be shown assignments that were assigned to him/her in the past. If during that past period the worker was unable to begin working on that assignment, the system may assign it to a different worker in the next shift or day.
