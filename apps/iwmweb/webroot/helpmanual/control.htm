Description:   

The Control section of IWM handles the daily activity monitoring and control in the system. It is designed as a command post for all activities managed by the system in real time. This is where all schedule and unscheduled work orders are managed either manually or automatically by the system automation agents. It is composed of five (5) main areas: Jobs, Projects, Mobile Worker, Maintenance Request, and Reports.

The IWM system constantly monitors the status and requirements of all objects in the system to automatically create and schedule work. A majority of this process is handled in the Control section which also serves as a report tool on the amount of work completed and pending.





The Control sub-sections of IWM:

·Jobs is a command sub-section where the management of work orders takes place (the only exception are Project Jobs).  

·Projects is a special command post for managing sequential work orders that have a specific end-goal and thus are grouped together to facilitate tracking.  

·Mobile Worker is the field component of IWM where work orders are provided to each worker individually either though a web-based portal or a hand-held device.  

·Maintenance Request is the sub-section of the system where unscheduled work orders or assessment requests are entered in the system. On important note to consider is that the distinction between schedule, unscheduled or projects jobs is transparent to the field worker. The combination of all types of jobs are automatically scheduled and assigned based on productivity optimization and not by source of work.
