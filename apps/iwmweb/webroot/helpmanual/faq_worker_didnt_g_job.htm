Worker did not get the job
Previous  Top  Next

Symptom: A job you desired was not given to a particular worker, or was not given to anyone.

Troubleshooting steps:

1.Note the Date, Location (highest level locator), Skill and Skill Level for that job.  
 
2.Verify that the job has Candidates.  
 
Comment: The system will only match those workers who meet the requirements for a job.  

3.   Verify that the job has been scheduled to the desired worker.

4.   Verify that the worker has a current schedule in display (see "No Jobs on Schedule").    

5.   Verify that the job's "Earliest Start Date" is not later than "today".
