Task:   Assigning access to the different sections of IWM to person.
   
Description:   Allows users to add, edit or remove the access to different sections in the system. This allows a person to completely manipulate the data in the section they have been granted access.    

1.Select the Worker  
 
Filter the hierarchy to find the person you desire from the "Worker List" screen.  
 
From the "Worker" screen, look for the person you wish to edit.  
 
2.Select the "Shield" button under the Rights column.  

This will display the "Rights" screen with the list of sections that a person may have access to, if any.  
 
     

3.   Check those sections you wish to grant access to the person by selecting the check boxes to the left of each section.

4.   Select the "save" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
