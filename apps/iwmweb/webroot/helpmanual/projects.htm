Description:   

Projects much like the Jobs subsection is a command section where the management of work orders takes place with the exception of only handling jobs that have been created under a 'project'. A 'project' is a group of jobs that have been created to achieve an ultimate goal and make-up the different tasking and ordering of tasking necessary to accomplish that goal. The one distinguishing feature of this sub-section is the systems capability track the project and its tasking automatically, so that work tasks follow the necessary chronological order needed to accomplish the desired project goal. The Projects sub-section has the same mechanisms and capabilities of the Jobs sub-section and is housed separately for simplification purposes and ease of use and tracking of progress. The Projects sub-section is where the activity defined in both the Requirements and Implementations sections takes place in the form or work orders. It is all handled automatically by the system from creation, planning, scheduling, assignment, and tracking of work. While the system is capable of carrying out all these activities, system users may override the automated agents and manage the work according to changing environmental conditions or expert judgment.
