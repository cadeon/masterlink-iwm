Remove Access
Previous  Top  Next

Task:   Removing an already assigned access to a section/s of IWM for a person.
   
Description:   Allows users to remove access to different sections in the system. This prohibits a person from manipulating any part of the data in the section/s they haven't been granted access.    

1.Select the Worker  
 
Filter the hierarchy to find the person you desire from the "Worker List" screen.  
 
From the "Worker" screen, look for the person you wish to edit.  
 
2.Select the "Rights" button under the Rights column.  

This will display the "Rights" screen with the list of sections that a person may have access to, if any.  
 
     

3.   Uncheck those sections you wish to remove access to the person by deselecting the check boxes to the right of each section.

4.   Select the "save" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
