Task:   Creating and Organization in IWM.
   
Description:   Allows the creation of a new independent Organization in the IWM system that houses human resources that share an overarching characteristic.    

1.Go to the Define Section of IWM  
 
2.Select the "Organizations" link . The system will display the "Organization List" screen.  
 
3.Select the "Add Organizations" button .  
 
The "Add Organization" screen will display overtop the organization page.  
 
4Complete the information required (name and type)  
 
 

5   Select the "save" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard the information.

   The system will then display the "Organizations" screen with the newly created organization at the end of the list.
