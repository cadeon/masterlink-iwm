Task:   Manually scheduling a Job.   

Description: Allows users to manually assign and schedule a job.  
 
1.Filter the hierarchy to find the job you wish to schedule in the "Job List" screen.  
 
2.Select the "Schdl" link for that job.  
 
This will display the "Job Schedule" screen.  

 

3.   Use the check box buttons to select from the candidate Workers available on the left, the one's to whom you wish to assign the job.

Note: Only those workers matching the requirements for that job will be displayed (Skill, Skill Level, Availability, Organization). The number of workers required will be determined by the Job properties as defined in the Template.  
 
4.Select the "Ok" button to record your changes, or select the "cancel" button to discard your changes.
