Description:   

"Skills" are a customizable set of capabilities that a person participating in the system may have. These are customized to the client's particular needs and may be edited at the client's request or changing needs. "Skills" play an important role in the system as they are used to match the task requirements with the person with the right capabilities. Thus a task is given a "Skill" requirement and a level of proficiency in that "Skill". The system will only consider and schedule work to those people who have the right skill and the minimum level of proficiency.
